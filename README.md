# German-onshore-wind-market-data-analysis

The repo analyses 10 year German onshore wind hourly data and represents finding on the capture price and its correlation with baseload market price and gas price. The assessment indicates a noticable difference pre and post Russia-Ukraine war in 2022 where onshore wind capture prices post war correlate much more strongly to TTF gas prices. This ultimately impacts the PPA floor pricing and merchant strategy in general for renewbales investors.
